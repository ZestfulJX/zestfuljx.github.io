About me
======

Welcome! I am Jiaxin Zhang(张佳鑫), a master student in CS at [Harbin Institute of Technology](https://www.hit.edu.cn/), supervised by [Prof. Junjun Jiang](https://homepage.hit.edu.cn/jiangjunjun). Currently, I am an intern at the Multimodal Model Lab of Huawei 2012 Laboratories, where I am mentored by [Dave Zhenyu Chen](https://daveredrum.github.io/).

My research interests focus on 3D reconstruction and vision-language model (VLM)-based 3D spatial perception and reasoning. Beyond research, I am a backpacker—either my body or my mind is always on the road.
